# TechNova Limited Warranty Policy

**Document ID:** WARRANTY-POL-001
**Version:** 2025.2
**Effective:** 2025-01-15
**Applies to:** TechNova-branded hardware sold globally (regional addenda may apply)

## 1. Standard Warranty Periods
| Product Category | Standard Warranty | Battery Warranty (if applicable) |
|---|---|---|
| Consumer laptops (NovaBook Air, NovaBook Pro) | 1 year limited hardware | 1 year |
| Premium laptops (NovaBook XPS) | 1 year limited hardware (Premium Support Plus 1 year included in select SKUs) | 1 year |
| Workstations (WorkStation Pro T-Series) | 3 years on-site (ProSupport) | N/A |
| Monitors (UltraView, ProView) | 3 years Advance Exchange | N/A |
| Enterprise Servers (NovaEdge) | 3 years ProSupport, 9x5 next-business-day on-site | N/A |
| Accessories (headsets, docks, mice, keyboards) | 1 year (Accessory Care: 3 years optional) | N/A (consumables not covered) |
| Backpacks and bags | 3 years TechNova Accessory Care | N/A |

## 2. Warranty Start Date
- Warranty starts on the **invoice date** of the original purchase from TechNova or an Authorized Reseller.
- If the invoice date is unavailable, warranty defaults to the **ship date + 30 days** from TechNova's manufacturing record.
- Customers can verify warranty status at `support.technova.com/warranty` using the Service Tag.

## 3. Warranty Service Levels
- **Carry-In / Mail-In Depot** (default for consumer): customer ships device to TechNova depot; 5-7 business day turnaround.
- **On-Site Service**: technician dispatched to customer location, typical for ProSupport and ProSupport Plus.
- **Advance Exchange** (monitors, select accessories): TechNova ships a replacement first; customer returns the defective unit within 10 calendar days.

## 4. What IS Covered
The Limited Warranty covers **manufacturing defects in materials and workmanship** during normal use, including:
- Failed motherboards, CPUs, RAM, SSDs, GPUs, displays (manufacturing defect).
- Failed batteries within the battery warranty period (capacity falls below 50% of design capacity within warranty).
- Failed power adapters and bundled cables.
- Dead-on-Arrival (DOA) defects within 30 days of delivery.

## 5. What Is NOT Covered
The Limited Warranty does **not** cover:
- **Accidental damage**: drops, spills, cracked screens, bent chassis, liquid ingress.
- Damage caused by **misuse**, including non-certified chargers or power surges without proper protection.
- **Cosmetic damage** that does not affect function (scratches, dents, fading).
- Damage from **unauthorized modifications** (third-party repair shops, opened tamper seals).
- **Consumables and wear items**: keyboard keycaps, palm rest paint, screen anti-reflective coatings, fan dust filters.
- **Software issues** unrelated to hardware: virus infection, OS corruption, user-installed application conflicts.
- Damage from **force majeure**: floods, fire, earthquakes, lightning strikes.
- Use in environments outside published operating specs.
- Loss or theft.

## 6. Optional Coverage Add-Ons
- **TechNova Accidental Damage Service (ADS)**: covers drops, spills, and cracked screens. Sold for 1, 2, 3, or 4 years.
- **Premium Support Plus (consumer)**: includes ADS + 24x7 priority phone + on-site service.
- **ProSupport Plus (commercial)**: ADS + Keep Your Hard Drive + predictive failure analysis + 24x7 mission-critical response on servers.
- **Battery Service Plan**: covers battery replacement once during plan duration regardless of degradation cause.

## 7. Battery Coverage Specifics
- Batteries are considered failed (and eligible for replacement) when Full Charge Capacity falls **below 50%** of Design Capacity within the warranty period.
- Battery swelling is **always** covered as a safety issue regardless of capacity reading, as long as the unit is within the battery warranty period or covered by ADS / Premium Support Plus.
- Reports of swelling must be treated as priority safety cases (see TS-LT-BAT-001 Safety Notice).

## 8. International Warranty
- TechNova warranty is honored in the country of original purchase.
- **International Travel Warranty** (paid add-on) extends coverage to a defined list of countries. Without it, customers traveling abroad must mail the device to a TechNova depot in the country of purchase.

## 9. Transferring Warranty
- Warranty is tied to the **Service Tag**, not the original purchaser.
- Customers selling or gifting a device can transfer ownership at `support.technova.com/transfer` to receive proactive service notifications.

## 10. Documentation Required for a Claim
- Service Tag.
- Proof of purchase (invoice or order number).
- Diagnostic report (auto-attached when the case is created from TechNova Companion or iNova).
- Photos for physical damage / battery swelling cases.

## 11. Out-of-Warranty Repair
Customers without active warranty can purchase **per-incident repair**:
- Diagnostic fee: $49 USD (waived if customer accepts repair quote).
- Parts at MSRP; labor billed in 30-minute increments.
- Out-of-warranty repairs carry a 90-day repair warranty on the parts replaced.

## 12. Regional Variations
- **EU**: 2-year legal conformity warranty applies in addition to the manufacturer's warranty.
- **India**: 1-year standard; certain SKUs ship with 3-year BIS-compliant warranty.
- **Australia**: Australian Consumer Law guarantees apply and are not limited by this policy.

For region-specific addenda, refer to `warranty-policy-{region}.md` documents.
