# TechNova Customer Support - Frequently Asked Questions

**Document ID:** FAQ-GEN-001
**Last Updated:** 2025-10-12

## Orders and Delivery

### Q1. How can I check the status of my order?
Sign in at `technova.com/orders`, or use the link in your shipping confirmation email. You can also ask the TechNova Support Assistant: "Where is my order #123456?" and we will look it up.

### Q2. How long does shipping take?
- Standard ground: 3-7 business days within the contiguous US.
- Expedited: 2 business days.
- Overnight: next business day for orders placed before 12:00 PM ET.
- Custom configurations add 3-5 business days for build time.

### Q3. Can I change my shipping address after I order?
You can change the address through `technova.com/orders` **before** the order enters the "Shipped" status. After shipment, contact the carrier directly. We cannot guarantee address changes once an order is in transit.

### Q4. My order shows delivered, but I did not receive it.
1. Check with neighbors and household members.
2. Confirm the delivery address on your invoice.
3. Wait 24 hours - carriers sometimes mark a package delivered before final drop-off.
4. If still missing, file a **carrier claim** through the link in your shipping email and contact TechNova; we will assist with the carrier investigation.

## Warranty and Service

### Q5. How do I check if my device is still under warranty?
Visit `support.technova.com/warranty` and enter the **Service Tag** (printed on the bottom of laptops, the rear of monitors, or the pull-out tag on workstations and servers).

### Q6. Does my warranty cover a cracked screen?
The standard limited warranty does **not** cover accidental damage such as cracked screens. Coverage requires **Accidental Damage Service (ADS)** or **Premium Support Plus**. Without those, repair is billable.

### Q7. My battery is swollen - is that covered?
Yes. Battery swelling is treated as a safety issue and is covered while the battery warranty is in effect (typically 1 year). **Stop using the device immediately**, disconnect from power, and contact support. Do not attempt to remove the battery yourself.

### Q8. How do I find my Service Tag?
- **NovaBook laptops**: Sticker on the bottom panel, or `Settings > System > About` (Windows), or in BIOS Setup (F2).
- **WorkStation Pro T-Series**: Pull-out information card at the front-top of the chassis.
- **NovaEdge servers**: Pull-out information tag at the front-left of the chassis, or in iNova > System Information.
- **Monitors**: Label on the back panel near the input ports.

## Returns and Refunds

### Q9. What is your return policy?
30 days from delivery for most consumer items (14 days for custom configurations and enterprise orders >= $5,000). See the full return policy for restocking fee rules and non-returnable items.

### Q10. How long does a refund take?
Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days after we receive the return at our return center, plus an additional 5-10 business days for the funds to appear on your original payment method.

### Q11. Can I return a customized laptop?
Yes, within 14 days of delivery, subject to a 15% restocking fee. The device must be in original condition with all included accessories.

## Setup and First Use

### Q12. How do I register my new device?
Connect to Wi-Fi during the Windows Out-of-Box Experience and follow the registration prompt, or register later at `technova.com/register` using your Service Tag.

### Q13. The function keys (F1-F12) do media controls instead of F-key actions. How do I switch them?
Press **Fn + Esc** to toggle Fn-Lock. To set the default behavior, change the **Function Key Behavior** option in `BIOS Setup (F2) > System Configuration > Function Key Behavior` to either "Function Key" or "Multimedia Key".

### Q14. My laptop says "Slow Charger" when I plug in. What does that mean?
The connected adapter cannot provide enough wattage. Use the bundled adapter (or another **TechNova-certified** USB-C PD adapter rated >= 100 W for the NovaBook XPS 15) for normal charging.

## Drivers and Software

### Q15. Where do I get the latest drivers?
Use **TechNova Companion** (pre-installed) or browse to `support.technova.com/drivers` and enter your Service Tag.

### Q16. I reinstalled Windows and lost all my drivers. What do I do?
See troubleshooting article TS-DRV-006. The short version: install **TechNova Companion** first, let it auto-detect drivers, install them in the recommended order (Chipset > Storage > Graphics > Network > Audio > others), then reboot.

### Q17. Is McAfee really needed? Can I uninstall it?
The 30-day McAfee LiveSafe trial is optional. Windows Defender provides baseline protection. You can uninstall McAfee via `Settings > Apps > Installed apps`.

## Proactive Notifications and Advisories

### Q18. How do I get notified about driver and security updates?
- Keep **TechNova Companion** installed; it surfaces critical updates automatically.
- Subscribe to **TechNova Security Advisories** at `support.technova.com/advisories` to receive emails for CVE and BIOS-related issues affecting your model.

### Q19. Where can I see known issues for my device model?
`support.technova.com/known-issues` lists open advisories per model. The TechNova Support Assistant can also search advisories on request: "Any known issues with the NovaBook XPS 15?"

## Contact and Channels

### Q20. How do I escalate an unresolved case?
Reply to any case email with the keyword `ESCALATE` in the subject, or ask a support agent: "Please escalate this to a senior agent." Enterprise customers with ProSupport Plus can use the dedicated 24x7 mission-critical hotline.

### Q21. What are TechNova's official support channels?
- Web chat and support portal: `support.technova.com`
- Phone (US/CA): 1-800-TECHNOVA (24x7 for ProSupport Plus and Premium Support Plus; 9x5 standard)
- Email: support@technova.com
- TechNova Support Assistant (in-product): in TechNova Companion, click **Get Support**.
