## 1. Product Overview
The TechNova NovaBook Pro 14 is a 14-inch business laptop balancing performance, portability, and battery life. It pairs 13th Gen Intel Core H-series processors with optional NVIDIA RTX 4050 discrete graphics in a 1.55 kg magnesium-aluminum chassis.

## 4. First-Time Setup
1. Connect the 100 W USB-C adapter to the left Thunderbolt 4 port and a grounded outlet.
2. Press the power button (top-right of keyboard) once. The status LED turns white.
3. Follow the Windows 11 Pro Out-of-Box Experience (OOBE).
4. Connect to Wi-Fi; the device pulls initial driver updates from TechNova Cloud during OOBE.
5. For corporate-managed devices, allow time for Intune/Autopilot enrollment to complete after sign-in.
6. Register your device at `technova.com/register` to activate your warranty.

## 5. Charging and Battery Care
- Full charge in approximately 75 minutes with the included 100 W adapter.
- Only use TechNova-certified 65 W or higher USB-C PD adapters. Lower-wattage chargers will trickle-charge and may show a "slow charger" notification. For discrete-GPU SKUs, sustained gaming or rendering on a 65 W adapter will gradually drain the battery.
- Expected battery life is 10-12 hours (iGPU) or 7-9 hours (dGPU) under mixed productivity workloads at 50% brightness.
- Enable **TechNova Battery Care** in the TechNova Companion app to cap charging at 80%.

## 6. Display Settings
- Default refresh rate: 60 Hz. Switch to 120 Hz under `Settings > System > Display > Advanced display`.
- Color profiles (sRGB, DCI-P3, Adobe RGB) are managed by the **TechNova Color Studio** application.
- Optional 10-point capacitive touch and pen input (Microsoft Pen Protocol 2.0); pen sold separately as TechNova Active Pen TP-100.

## 7. Keyboard, Touchpad, and Biometric Login
- Spill-resistant backlit keyboard with three brightness levels (Fn + F5 / F6).
- Precision Touchpad with multi-finger gestures and haptic click option (optional SKU).
- Fingerprint reader integrated in the power button.
- IR camera supports Windows Hello face login (1080p webcam standard, optional 5MP webcam with computational privacy).

## 8. Software Pre-installed
- Windows 11 Pro (64-bit)
- TechNova Companion
- TechNova Color Studio
- TechNova Power Manager

## 9. Driver and BIOS Updates
- Open the **TechNova Companion** app and select **Check for Updates**.
- For Intune-managed fleets, BIOS and firmware updates can be pushed via the **TechNova Update Catalog for Intune**.
- Service Tag is on a sticker on the bottom of the laptop and in `System Information > BIOS > Service Tag`.

## 10. Safety and Compliance
- Operating temperature: 0°C to 35°C.
- Storage temperature: -20°C to 60°C.
- Do not block the rear ventilation grills; allow at least 8 cm of clearance.
- Complies with FCC Part 15, CE, BIS, and RoHS.

## 11. Common Indicator Lights
| LED | Color | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Power | Solid white | System is on |
| Power | Pulsing white | Sleep / modern standby |
| Power | Solid amber | Battery critically low |
| Power | Blinking amber (3 short, 1 long) | Memory failure - run diagnostics |
| Power | Blinking amber (4 short, 1 long) | Storage failure - run diagnostics |
| Charging | Solid white | Charging in progress |

## 12. SIM Tray (5G WWAN SKUs only)
- Nano-SIM tray located on the right side near the rear vent.
- Use the included SIM ejector tool (or a paperclip) to release the tray.
- eSIM is supported in parallel with the physical nano-SIM (dual-SIM dual-standby).
- Configure cellular profiles under `Settings > Network & Internet > Cellular`.

## 13. Compatibility Notes

- UltraView U2724Q monitor: 4K @ 120 Hz over a single USB-C cable; KVM passes the laptop keyboard and trackpad to a second host when configured.
- TechNova Premier Wireless Headset HS-720 and Precision Mouse MS-500 are tuned for the NovaBook Pro 14 with low-latency 2.4 GHz dongle support.
