## 1. Catalog Overview
The TechNova mouse lineup covers wired entry, wireless mainstream, ergonomic vertical, and creator/precision tiers. All wireless TechNova mice support TechNova Easy-Switch (pair up to 3 devices and switch with a single button) and are managed by **TechNova Peripheral Manager (TPM)** for button remapping and firmware updates.

The detailed manual for the **TechNova Precision Mouse MS-500** is published in MAN-ACC-CAT-2025 (accessories.md). This document covers the rest of the lineup.

### First-time pairing (MS-300)
1. Insert the AA battery (orientation diagram inside the battery cover).
2. Slide the power switch to **On**.
3. For Bluetooth: hold the connect button for 3 seconds until the LED flashes blue; pair as `TechNova MS-300`.
4. For dongle: plug the dongle into any USB-A port; it auto-pairs.
5. Easy-Switch: short-press the connect button to cycle between paired hosts.

## 8. Driver and Firmware Updates
- Open **TechNova Peripheral Manager (TPM)** on Windows or macOS.
- Connect the mouse via USB (cable or dongle) for firmware updates. Firmware updates over Bluetooth are **not** supported.

## 9. Common Issues and Resolutions
| Symptom | Likely cause | Action |
|---|---|---|
| Cursor stutters or skips | Low battery, surface reflectivity | Replace AA (MS-300/500) or charge via USB-C (MS-700/880); use a mouse pad on glass surfaces. |
| Mouse disconnects when Wi-Fi active | 2.4 GHz interference with dongle | Switch to Bluetooth, or move the dongle to a USB extension cable away from the Wi-Fi adapter. |
| Easy-Switch not switching hosts | Host out of Bluetooth range | Make sure the previously paired host is within 10 m; otherwise short-press connect again. |
| MagSpeed wheel won't toggle modes (MS-880) | Mode button mapped to a custom action in TPM | Open TPM > Mouse > Buttons > restore mode button to default. |
