## 1. Product Overview
The TechNova NovaBook XPS 15 is a 15.6-inch premium laptop designed for creative professionals, developers, and power users. It ships with a 13th Gen Intel Core i7/i9 processor, NVIDIA RTX 4060 discrete graphics, up to 64 GB DDR5 RAM, and up to 2 TB NVMe Gen4 SSD storage.

## 4. First-Time Setup
1. Connect the 130 W USB-C power adapter to the rear Thunderbolt 4 port and a grounded outlet.
2. Press the power button (top-right of keyboard) once. The status LED turns white.
3. Follow the Windows 11 Out-of-Box Experience (OOBE): select region, language, keyboard layout.
4. Connect to Wi-Fi; the device will pull initial driver updates from TechNova Cloud during OOBE.
5. Sign in with a Microsoft account (recommended) or create a local account.
6. When prompted, register your device at `technova.com/register` to activate your warranty.

## 5. Charging and Battery Care
- The device fully charges in approximately 80 minutes using the included 130 W adapter.
- Only use TechNova-certified 100 W or higher USB-C PD adapters. Lower-wattage chargers will trickle-charge and may show a "slow charger" notification.
- For optimal battery longevity, keep charge between 20% and 80%. Enable **TechNova Battery Care** in the TechNova Companion app to cap charging at 80%.
- The expected battery life is 8-10 hours under mixed productivity workloads at 50% brightness.

## 6. Display Settings
- Default refresh rate: 60 Hz. Switch to 120 Hz under `Settings > System > Display > Advanced display`.
- Color profiles (sRGB, DCI-P3, Adobe RGB) are managed by the **TechNova Color Studio** application.
- HDR is supported on the OLED panel; toggle under `Settings > System > Display > HDR`.

## 7. Keyboard, Touchpad, and Biometric Login
- Backlit keyboard with three brightness levels (Fn + F5 / F6).
- Precision Touchpad with multi-finger gestures (Windows Settings > Bluetooth & devices > Touchpad).
- Fingerprint reader integrated in the power button. Enroll under `Settings > Accounts > Sign-in options > Fingerprint recognition (Windows Hello)`.
- IR camera supports Windows Hello face login.

## 8. Software Pre-installed
- Windows 11 Pro (64-bit)
- TechNova Companion (driver and BIOS updates, support tools)
- TechNova Color Studio
- TechNova Power Manager
- McAfee LiveSafe trial (30 days)

## 9. Driver and BIOS Updates
- Open the **TechNova Companion** app and select **Check for Updates**.
- Updates can also be downloaded manually from `support.technova.com/drivers` using the device service tag.
- The service tag is printed on a sticker on the bottom of the laptop and is also available under `System Information > BIOS > Service Tag`.

## 10. Safety and Compliance
- Operating temperature: 0°C to 35°C (32°F to 95°F).
- Storage temperature: -20°C to 60°C (-4°F to 140°F).
- Do not block the rear ventilation grills. Allow at least 10 cm of clearance when in use on a desk.
- The device complies with FCC Part 15, CE, BIS, and RoHS.

## 11. Common Indicator Lights
| LED | Color | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Power | Solid white | System is on |
| Power | Pulsing white | Sleep / modern standby |
| Power | Solid amber | Battery critically low |
| Power | Blinking amber (3 short, 1 long) | Memory failure - run diagnostics |
| Charging | Solid white | Charging in progress |
| Charging | Off | Fully charged or no power adapter detected |
