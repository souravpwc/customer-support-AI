## 1. TechNova Premier Wireless Headset HS-720

| Spec | Value |
|---|---|
| Connectivity | Bluetooth 5.3, 2.4 GHz USB-A dongle, USB-C wired |
| Battery | Up to 50 hours (Bluetooth, ANC off); 30 hours (ANC on) |
| Charging | USB-C, full charge in 90 minutes; 10-minute quick charge = 6 hours |
| Microphone | Retractable boom, AI noise suppression |
| Certifications | Microsoft Teams, Zoom |

**First-time pairing**
1. Slide the power switch to **On**. Hold the **Bluetooth** button 3 seconds until the LED flashes blue/red.
2. On your computer or phone, scan for Bluetooth devices and select `TechNova HS-720`.
3. To switch to USB dongle mode, slide the source switch on the right ear cup from **BT** to **Dongle**.

## 2. TechNova Thunderbolt 4 Dock TD-340

| Spec | Value |
|---|---|
| Host connection | Thunderbolt 4 (40 Gbps) |
| Power delivery | 130 W to host |
| Displays | Up to 3x 4K @ 60 Hz, or 1x 8K @ 60 Hz, or 2x 4K @ 144 Hz |
| Ports | 2x TB4 downstream, 4x USB-A 3.2 Gen 2, 1x USB-C 3.2 Gen 2, 2x DisplayPort 1.4, 1x 2.5 GbE, audio combo |
| OS support | Windows 11, macOS 13+, Linux (kernel 5.15+) |

**Notes**
- For full 130 W PD to the host, use the included 180 W power brick. Third-party bricks may downgrade PD to 65–96 W.
- For correct multi-monitor wake from sleep on Windows, install **TechNova Dock Firmware Updater** and ensure firmware ≥ `1.4.7`.

## 6. Driver and Firmware Updates
- Open **TechNova Peripheral Manager (TPM)** on Windows or macOS.
- Connect the accessory directly to the computer via USB (wired) for firmware updates. Firmware updates over Bluetooth are not supported.
