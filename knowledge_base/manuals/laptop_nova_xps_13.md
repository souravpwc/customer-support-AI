## 1. Product Overview
The TechNova NovaBook XPS 13 is a 13.4-inch premium ultraportable laptop designed for executives and frequent travelers who want flagship build quality and a 3.5K InfinityEdge OLED option in a 1.18 kg machined aluminum chassis. It pairs Intel Core Ultra 7 / Ultra 9 processors with integrated Arc Graphics and zero-bezel display.

## 4. First-Time Setup
1. Connect the 60 W USB-C adapter to either Thunderbolt 4 port and a grounded outlet.
2. Press the power button (integrated capacitive function row, top-right). The status LED turns white.
3. Follow the Windows 11 Pro Out-of-Box Experience (OOBE).
4. Connect to Wi-Fi; the device pulls initial driver updates from TechNova Cloud during OOBE.
5. Sign in with a Microsoft account (recommended) or create a local account.
6. Register your device at `technova.com/register` to activate your warranty and Premium Support Plus (if included).

## 5. Charging and Battery Care
- Full charge in approximately 65 minutes with the included 60 W adapter.
- Only use TechNova-certified 45 W or higher USB-C PD adapters. Lower-wattage chargers will trickle-charge and may show a "slow charger" notification.
- Expected battery life: 14-16 hours (FHD+) or 9-11 hours (OLED) under mixed productivity at 50% brightness.
- Enable **TechNova Battery Care** in the TechNova Companion app to cap charging at 80% for longer battery lifespan.

## 6. Capacitive Function Row
- The XPS 13 replaces the traditional function key row with a **touch-sensitive LED function row** that toggles between Fn keys and media controls.
- Press **Fn + Esc** to toggle the row mode (Function ↔ Media).
- The row is lit at all times the lid is open; brightness adjusts via Fn + F5 / F6 (when in Media mode the slider appears on screen).

## 7. Zero-Lattice Keyboard and Touchpad
- Edge-to-edge keys with 1.0 mm travel and per-key backlight (3 brightness levels).
- Force-sensitive glass haptic touchpad (no visible borders); set haptic intensity under `Settings > Bluetooth & devices > Touchpad > TechNova Haptic`.
- Fingerprint reader integrated in the power button (capacitive). IR camera for Windows Hello face login.

## 8. Display Settings (OLED only)
- Default refresh rate: 60 Hz (OLED panel does not support higher refresh).
- HDR True Black 500 certified; toggle under `Settings > System > Display > HDR`.
- Built-in pixel-shift and screen saver to mitigate burn-in - leave both enabled.
- Color profiles (sRGB, DCI-P3, Adobe RGB) are managed by **TechNova Color Studio**.

## 10. Driver and BIOS Updates
- Open the **TechNova Companion** app and select **Check for Updates**.
- Manual driver downloads at `support.technova.com/drivers` using the Service Tag.
- Service Tag is laser-etched on the bottom of the laptop and in `System Information > BIOS > Service Tag`.

## 11. Safety and Compliance
- Operating temperature: 0°C to 35°C.
- Storage temperature: -20°C to 60°C.
- Do not block the bottom intake vents; use a hard, flat surface.
- Complies with FCC Part 15, CE, BIS, and RoHS. EPEAT Gold (FHD+) / EPEAT Silver (OLED).

## 12. Common Indicator Lights
| LED | Color | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Power | Solid white | System is on |
| Power | Pulsing white | Sleep / modern standby |
| Power | Solid amber | Battery critically low |
| Power | Blinking amber (3 short, 1 long) | Memory failure - run diagnostics |
| Charging | Solid white | Charging in progress |
| Charging | Off | Fully charged or no power adapter detected |
