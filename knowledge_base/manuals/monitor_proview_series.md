## 1. Series Overview
The TechNova ProView lineup provides essential business displays at attractive price points. All ProView monitors include:
- IPS panel with anti-glare coating.
- ComfortView Plus low blue light (TÜV Rheinland Eyesafe certified).
- Flicker-free backlight.
- VESA 100 x 100 mm mounting (P-Series and above).
- 3-year Advance Exchange warranty.

ProView monitors do **not** include USB-C PD, KVM, or daisy-chaining; for those, refer to the UltraView lineup (MAN-MON-UV-SER-2025).

## 5. First-Time Setup (All ProView)
1. Attach the stand: align hooks at the rear, press until the latch clicks.
2. Connect a video cable: DisplayPort or HDMI to host.
3. (Optional) Connect the USB-B upstream cable to use the built-in USB hub.
4. Power on with the joystick on the lower-right rear.
5. Use the joystick to select input: **Input Source > DP / HDMI / VGA**.

## 6. Daisy-Chaining
Daisy-chaining is **not** supported on ProView models. For MST chaining, use the UltraView U2724Q, U2725D, U3225QE, or U4025QW.

## 7. Firmware Updates
- Connect via USB-B upstream and run **TechNova Display Manager (TDM)** > Updates.
- ProView monitors typically receive firmware updates only once per generation, focused on EDID and Energy Star compliance.

## 8. Indicator LED
| LED | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Off | Monitor off |
| Solid white | On, signal received |
| Blinking white | Standby / no signal |
| Solid amber | Firmware update in progress |

## 9. Cleaning
- Use a soft, lint-free microfiber cloth.
- For stubborn marks, lightly dampen the cloth with distilled water. Do not spray liquids directly on the panel.

## 10. Warranty
ProView monitors carry a **3-year Advance Exchange** warranty (see WARRANTY-POL-001). Pixel-defect policy is identical to UltraView: replacement on 3+ bright or 5+ dark sub-pixel defects.
