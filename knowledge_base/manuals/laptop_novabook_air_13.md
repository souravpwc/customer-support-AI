## 1. Product Overview
The TechNova NovaBook Air 13 is a 13.3-inch ultraportable laptop designed for travel, classroom, and everyday productivity. It pairs Intel Core Ultra 5/7 processors with a fanless or low-fan thermal design, all in a 1.20 kg aluminum chassis.

## 4. First-Time Setup
1. Connect the 65 W USB-C adapter to either Thunderbolt 4 port and a grounded outlet.
2. Press the power button (top-right of keyboard) once. The status LED turns white.
3. Follow the Windows 11 Home/Pro Out-of-Box Experience (OOBE).
4. Connect to Wi-Fi; the device pulls initial driver updates from TechNova Cloud during OOBE.
5. Sign in with a Microsoft account (recommended) or create a local account.
6. Register your device at `technova.com/register` to activate your warranty.

## 5. Charging and Battery Care
- Full charge in approximately 70 minutes with the included 65 W adapter.
- Only use TechNova-certified 45 W or higher USB-C PD adapters. Lower-wattage chargers will trickle-charge and may show a "slow charger" notification.
- Expected battery life is 12-15 hours under mixed productivity workloads at 50% brightness.
- Enable **TechNova Battery Care** in the TechNova Companion app to cap charging at 80% for longer battery lifespan.

## 6. Display Settings
- Default refresh rate: 60 Hz. The panel does not support higher refresh rates.
- Color profile: sRGB (factory calibrated, ΔE < 2).
- Brightness up to 400 nits; recommended outdoor brightness setting is 80-100%.

## 7. Keyboard, Touchpad, and Biometric Login
- Spill-resistant backlit keyboard with two brightness levels (Fn + F5 / F6).
- Precision Touchpad with multi-finger gestures.
- Fingerprint reader integrated in the power button. Enroll under `Settings > Accounts > Sign-in options > Fingerprint recognition`.
- 1080p HD webcam with electronic privacy shutter (Fn + F10).

## 8. Software Pre-installed
- Windows 11 Home (default) or Windows 11 Pro (selectable at order)
- TechNova Companion
- TechNova Battery Care
- McAfee LiveSafe trial (30 days)

## 9. Driver and BIOS Updates
- Open the **TechNova Companion** app and select **Check for Updates**.
- Manual driver downloads at `support.technova.com/drivers` using the Service Tag.
- Service Tag is printed on a sticker on the bottom of the laptop.

## 10. Safety and Compliance
- Operating temperature: 0°C to 35°C.
- Storage temperature: -20°C to 60°C.
- Do not block the bottom intake vents; use a hard, flat surface.
- The device complies with FCC Part 15, CE, BIS, and RoHS.

## 11. Common Indicator Lights
| LED | Color | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Power | Solid white | System is on |
| Power | Pulsing white | Sleep / modern standby |
| Power | Solid amber | Battery critically low |
| Charging | Solid white | Charging in progress |
| Charging | Off | Fully charged or no power adapter detected |

## 12. Compatibility Notes
- Compatible with TechNova Thunderbolt 4 Dock TD-340 (single-cable docking).
- Power delivery via TB4 dock: up to 65 W. For workloads that draw more than 35 W sustained, prefer the included 65 W adapter.
- Compatible with all TechNova UltraView and ProView monitors via USB-C / TB4 (up to 1x 4K @ 60 Hz or 2x QHD @ 60 Hz daisy-chained over MST).
