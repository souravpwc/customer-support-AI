## 1. Series Overview
The TechNova UltraView lineup targets color-critical, multi-display, and high-density-workflow customers. All UltraView monitors include:
- IPS Black or OLED panels with factory color calibration (ΔE < 2).
- USB-C power delivery for single-cable docking with laptops.
- Built-in KVM for two-source switching.
- 3-year Advance Exchange warranty (see WARRANTY-POL-001).
- VESA 100 x 100 mm mounting.

The detailed manual for the **UltraView U2724Q 27" 4K** is published as document MAN-MON-U2724Q-2025. This document covers the rest of the lineup.

### First-Time Setup (U3225QE)
1. Attach the stand: align hooks at the rear, press until the latch clicks.
2. Connect a single Thunderbolt 4 cable to a compatible laptop (NovaBook Pro 14, NovaBook XPS 15, WorkStation Pro T-Series).
3. Power on with the joystick on the lower-right rear.
4. The monitor delivers up to 140 W to the host, supports 2.5 GbE wired network passthrough, and exposes 4x USB-A + 1x USB-C downstream as a hub.
5. For non-TB4 hosts, use DisplayPort or HDMI; USB hub functionality requires the USB-C upstream cable in addition.

### Daisy-Chaining (U3225QE)
- Connect a TB4 cable from the U3225QE's **TB4 downstream** port to a second TB4 monitor's TB4 in.
- Maximum chain via TB4: 2x 4K @ 60 Hz, or 1x 4K @ 120 Hz + 1x QHD @ 60 Hz.

## 5. Firmware Updates (All UltraView Models)
- Connect the monitor to your computer via USB-C or USB-B upstream.
- Run **TechNova Display Manager (TDM)** > Updates.
- Updates take 4-6 minutes. Do not disconnect power during update; the monitor will retry on next boot if interrupted.

## 6. Indicator LED (All UltraView Models)
| LED | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Off | Monitor off |
| Solid white | On, signal received |
| Blinking white | Standby / no signal |
| Solid amber | Firmware update in progress |
| Blinking amber (slow) | Recoverable firmware error - power cycle |

## 7. Cleaning
- Use a soft, lint-free microfiber cloth.
- For stubborn marks, lightly dampen the cloth with distilled water or a 50/50 isopropyl alcohol mix. Do not spray liquids directly on the panel.

## 8. Warranty
All UltraView monitors carry a **3-year Advance Exchange** warranty (see WARRANTY-POL-001 section 1 and 3). Pixel-defect policy: TechNova ships an Advance Exchange replacement if 3 or more bright sub-pixel defects or 5 or more dark sub-pixel defects are confirmed.
