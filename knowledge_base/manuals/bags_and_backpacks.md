## 1. Catalog Overview
The TechNova carry lineup covers everyday slim backpacks, executive briefcases, large-capacity ProBackpacks, and 4-wheel travel rollers. All TechNova bags include:
- Padded laptop compartment with brushed-fleece lining (no scratch surface).
- YKK zippers throughout.
- TechNova RFID-blocking accessory pocket.
- TechNova Accessory Care warranty (3 years, see WARRANTY-POL-001 section 1).

The detailed manual for the **TechNova ProBackpack 17"** is published in MAN-ACC-CAT-2025 (accessories.md). This document covers the rest of the lineup.

## 7. Care and Cleaning
- **Polyester / nylon bags**: spot-clean with a soft cloth and mild soap; air-dry away from direct sun.
- **Leather briefcase**: wipe with a dry microfiber cloth weekly; apply TechNova Leather Conditioner LC-100 every 6 months.
- Do **not** machine wash or tumble dry any TechNova bag - this voids the warranty (see WARRANTY-POL-001 section 5 "Damage caused by misuse").

## 8. Warranty
All TechNova bags and backpacks carry a **3-year TechNova Accessory Care** warranty covering manufacturing defects in materials and workmanship (zippers, stitching, frame, wheels for the Travel Roller). See WARRANTY-POL-001. Wear-and-tear (cosmetic scuffing, normal abrasion of straps, color fading) is not covered.

## 9. Returns
Bags and backpacks follow the standard consumer return policy: 30 calendar days from delivery, no restocking fee if unopened, 15% restocking fee if opened with all tags removed. See RETURN-POL-001.
