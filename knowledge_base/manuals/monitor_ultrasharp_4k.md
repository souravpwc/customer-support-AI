## 1. Product Overview
The TechNova UltraView U2724Q is a 27-inch 4K UHD (3840 x 2160) IPS Black monitor featuring 120 Hz refresh, USB-C 90 W power delivery, and a built-in KVM for two-source switching.

## 5. First-Time Setup
1. Place the monitor on a flat surface. The stand snaps into the back; align the hooks and press until it clicks.
2. Connect a video cable (DisplayPort or USB-C recommended for 120 Hz).
3. Connect the power cable. Power on with the joystick on the lower right rear.
4. Use the joystick to navigate the OSD: Input Source > select the connected port.
5. For USB-C single-cable setup with a laptop: enable **USB-C Prioritization** under `OSD > Display > USB-C Prioritization > High Resolution` for full 4K @ 120 Hz, or **High Data Speed** for USB 3.2 Gen2 data.

## 6. KVM and Picture-by-Picture
- Built-in KVM switches USB peripherals between two video inputs.
- Configure: `OSD > USB > KVM Setup`. Assign USB-C upstream to Source 1, USB-B upstream to Source 2.
- Use the joystick shortcut: **right-press** to toggle KVM source.

## 7. Daisy-Chaining (DisplayPort MST)
- Connect a DisplayPort cable from your computer to **DP IN** on the monitor.
- Connect a second DisplayPort cable from **DP OUT** to the second monitor's DP IN.
- Set **OSD > Display > MST > On** on both monitors.
- Maximum chain: 2x 4K @ 60 Hz, or 4x 1440p @ 60 Hz.

## 8. Firmware Updates
- Connect the monitor to your computer via USB-C or USB-B upstream.
- Run **TechNova Display Manager (TDM)** > Updates.
- Firmware updates take 4-6 minutes. Do not disconnect power during the update.

## 9. Indicator LED
| LED | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Off | Monitor off |
| Solid white | On, signal received |
| Blinking white | Standby / no signal |
| Solid amber | Firmware update in progress |

## 10. Cleaning
- Use a soft, lint-free microfiber cloth.
- For stubborn marks, lightly dampen the cloth with distilled water or a 50/50 isopropyl alcohol mix. Do not spray liquids directly on the panel.
