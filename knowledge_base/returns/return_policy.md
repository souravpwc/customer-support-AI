# TechNova Return and Refund Policy

**Document ID:** RETURN-POL-001
**Version:** 2025.2
**Effective:** 2025-01-15
**Applies to:** Direct purchases from `technova.com` and TechNova-operated retail stores. Authorized Reseller purchases follow reseller return policy.

## 1. Return Window
| Purchase Type | Return Window | Restocking Fee |
|---|---|---|
| Consumer hardware (laptops, monitors, accessories) | 30 calendar days from delivery | None for unopened; up to 15% for opened |
| Small business orders (< $5,000) | 30 calendar days | 15% on opened items |
| Enterprise orders (>= $5,000 or > 10 units) | 14 calendar days, with prior RMA approval | Negotiated per contract |
| Software, digital downloads, subscriptions | Non-returnable after activation | N/A |
| Customized configurations (CTO laptops, custom servers) | 14 calendar days; subject to inspection | 15% standard, may be higher |
| Consumables (batteries, toner, cleaning kits) | 30 days, unopened only | None |
| Gift recipients | 30 days from delivery, store credit | Same as above |

## 2. Eligibility Requirements
To be eligible for a refund or exchange the item must:
- Be within the applicable return window.
- Be in original condition with all included accessories (adapters, cables, manuals, documentation).
- Be in the original packaging where reasonably possible.
- Have all data wiped (see Data Erasure section) before shipping.
- Not be a non-returnable item (see Non-Returnable Items section).

## 3. Non-Returnable Items
- Customized or engraved products.
- Activated software licenses, subscriptions, digital downloads.
- Opened consumables: thermal paste, cleaning solutions.
- Gift cards.
- Products marked "Final Sale" or "Open-Box Clearance" in the cart.
- Products damaged by the customer after delivery.

## 4. How to Start a Return
1. Sign in at `technova.com/orders` and select the order.
2. Click **Start a Return** next to the item; choose a reason (defective, wrong product, no longer needed, etc.).
3. Print the prepaid return label (free for defective items and DOA; deducted from refund for change-of-mind on opened items).
4. Pack securely in the original box.
5. Drop off at the carrier within 7 days of label creation.

## 5. Refund Processing
- Inspection: 3-5 business days after receipt at the return center.
- Refund issued to **original payment method** within 5-10 additional business days. Banks may take additional days to post.
- Gift returns: store credit only.

## 6. DOA (Dead-On-Arrival) Replacements
- Reports within **30 days of delivery** that confirm a hardware defect are treated as **DOA**.
- DOA cases receive **free Advance Exchange**: TechNova ships a replacement first; customer returns the defective unit in the same box with the included prepaid label.
- DOA does NOT cover accidental damage; if damage is detected at inspection, the case is reclassified as billable.

## 7. Exchanges
- For exchanges to a **same SKU**, process as a DOA or warranty replacement.
- For exchanges to a **different SKU**, customer must place a new order and return the original under standard return rules.

## 8. Data Erasure Before Return
Customers are responsible for backing up and erasing personal data before returning a device. TechNova provides:
- **TechNova SecureWipe** utility (free, downloadable from `support.technova.com/securewipe`) that runs an NVMe Sanitize / ATA Secure Erase.
- For BitLocker-encrypted drives, simply removing the recovery key and reinstalling Windows is acceptable.
- TechNova is **not liable** for data left on returned devices.

## 9. Restocking Fees - Detailed
- **No fee** for: unopened items, defective items, DOA, TechNova shipping error, wrong item shipped.
- **15% restocking fee** for: opened consumer hardware returned for change-of-mind reasons.
- **Higher fees** may apply for custom-configured items at TechNova's discretion (disclosed before order).

## 10. Damaged or Lost Returns in Transit
- If a return is lost or damaged in transit using the TechNova-provided label, TechNova absorbs the loss.
- If the customer uses a non-TechNova carrier, the customer is responsible for proof of delivery and damage in transit.

## 11. International Returns
- Returns are accepted from the country of original purchase only.
- Cross-border returns are not supported; customers should contact TechNova International Sales for advice.

## 12. Pricing Adjustments
- If a returned item has dropped in price within 14 days of delivery, customers may request a one-time **price match** instead of returning the item.

## 13. Sample Customer-Facing Scripts

### "I want to return an unopened accessory I bought 2 weeks ago."
> "Of course - that's well within our 30-day window. Since it is unopened, there is no restocking fee. I will create a prepaid return label and email it to you. You should see the refund on your original payment method within 5-10 business days after we receive the package."

### "My laptop arrived with a dead pixel cluster."
> "I am sorry to hear that. Since the device is within 30 days of delivery and shows a hardware defect, this qualifies as Dead-on-Arrival. I will set up a free Advance Exchange - we ship the replacement first, and you ship the original back in the same box using the included prepaid label."

### "I customized a workstation and want to return it - I just changed my mind."
> "Customized configurations have a 14-day return window and a 15% restocking fee. Would you still like to proceed?"
